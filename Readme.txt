Steps to Run the Project
______________________________________

1. Use laptop or a Power Bank to give power supply to ESP8266 via USB Cable.
2. Ensure your mobile hotspot is turned on with these setting and an active internet connection (SSID: "Test", Password: "12345678").
3. Wait for the WIFI of ESP8266 to connect with your hotspot.
4. Wait for GPS module to connect with the satellite, once it is connected you will se a continuous Red light blinking on the Module. It generally takes 2 minutes to connect but if the signal is weak move to an open area like balcony or near a window where antenna of GPS module facing to the open sky. (Concrete walls can block the GPS signal). It can take 10-12 minutes when it is on a new place.
5. Once the Red light on the GPS starts blinking, it means your GPS module is connected with the satellite and your project is ready to use.
6. Press the Push Button once: It will turn on the buzzer and the LED for 3 seconds.
7. Press the Push Button Twice: It will send the signal to thingspeak. 
After pressing the Push Button Twice you will hear a beep sound after minor delay. If you hear the beep sound it means data successfully sent to thingspeak.
8. Open the file "LiveTracker.html" to view the live location of the GPS module.
